You can't go wrong with a classic hotdog.

Description

I'll admit it, I'm being a lazy here and adding a simple recipe to save time. That being said, you can't go wrong with a classic hotdog. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 hot dog bun
  • 1 hot dog
  • Ketchup (optional)
  • Mustard (optional)
  • Relish (optional)
  • Any other toppings that strike your fancy

Steps

  1. Cook hotdog using your preferred method: on a barbecue, on a frying pan, in the microwave, in boiling water, over a campfire, etc.
  2. Place the cooked hotdog in the hot dog bun.
  3. Add your favorite condiments and toppings.
  4. Eat!!!
